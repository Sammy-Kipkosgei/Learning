      Ingredients

    1. 1 ablespoon vegetable oil
    2. 1 small onion, scrubbed with clean vegetable brush under running water and sliced
    3. 1 pound beef, pork, chicken, shrimp, or tofu, cubed or thinly sliced*
    4. 1 (14 ounce) package frozen oriental vegetables, thawed or 4 cups fresh vegetables, chopped (such as zucchini, broccoli, cabbage, mushrooms, etc.)
    5. ¾ cup water
    6. 1 Tablespoon cornstarch
    7. 1 teaspoon lemon juice
    8. 1 teaspoon suga
    9. 2 Tablespoons soy sauce
    10. 2 cups brown rice, cooked

      Recipe

    1. Wash hands with soap and water.
    2. In a large skillet, heat oil. Sauté onion for 1 minute.
    3. Add protein food of choice. Stir-fry until nearly cooked through. Add vegetables. Cook for 2 to 4 minutes, adding water if needed. Continue cooking until meat is cooked throug
    4. In a separate bowl, mix together water, cornstarch, lemon juice, sugar, and soy sauce. Mix well.)
    5. ¾Pour mixture over vegetables. Heat 2 to 3 minutes or until thickened
    6. Serve over cooked rice.
    7. ore leftovers in a sealed container in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.
