How to cook Ugali

Requirements

  • Water
  • Flour
  • Sufuria
  • Mwiko
  1. Put some water on the sufuria
  2. Heat water for 15minutes to boil
  3. put some flour in the bpoiling water as you stire them
  4. Keep adding as you stir untill ugali ikue ugali
  5. Keep on low heat for 10min
  6. Serve it hot
